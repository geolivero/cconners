Chrystal Conners

This true showgirl started in the late 90 as “Desire”.A couple of years ago Desire changed into “Chrystal Conners”. Everybody knows that “Chrystal Conners” is known for her powerful shows and international allure. If you think that “Chrystal Conners” is the basic dragqueen than think again. With her dancers she takes you to a whole different level of dragness. She also released her debute single Superstar and is now working with amazing producers on her first album. With her amazing looks she tries to get you out of the real world and take you to a world that is not from here. She is also known for her jobs as creative producer, dancer, host, song writer, business woman, designer and for organising party’s. With her little army of creative people “Chrystal Conners” tries to put Dragness in a whole different light. This is not the end this is just the beginning. Step into my world and let yourself go.
